--- layout: doc/layouts/default.html --- # Grid

Create powerful multi-device layouts quickly and easily with the 12-column, nestable Foundation grid. If you're familiar with grid systems, you'll feel right at home. If not, you'll learn quickly.

***

Basic

Start by adding an element with a class of `row`, forming your block element. Then add divs with a `columns` class within that row with and specify the widths of those inline elements with the `small-#`, `medium-#`, and `large-#` classes. * **Foundation uses border-box**: Borders and padding do not affect the overall width of the columns, making the math dead-simple. * **Foundation is mobile-first**. Code for small first, and larger devices will inherit those styles. Customize for larger screens as necessary.

{{> examples_grid_offsets_rendered}} *** ##### Centered Columns Center your columns by adding a class of `small-centered` to your `column`. Large will inherit small centering by default, but you can also center on large only by applying a `large-centered` class. To uncenter on large screens use `large-uncentered`.

{{> examples_grid_centered_rendered}} *** ##### Source Ordering Using these source ordering classes, you can shift columns around between our breakpoints. This means you can have a sub-nav on the left on large displays, but go below on small ones.
